About Zentaka

Zentaka is a multinational cybernetics and defense conglomerate specializing in human augmentation, neural interfacing, and advanced bio-mechanical systems. With headquarters in the Neo-Tokyo Arcology and operations in over 80 territories, Zentaka remains at the forefront of post-human innovation, seamlessly integrating man and machine.

Founded in 2047 by visionary industrialist Epsi, the company rose to prominence through exclusive military contracts and rapid advancements in neural mesh technology. Instrumental to Zentaka's cutting-edge developments is , a master schematics engineer whose designs have shaped the next generation of cybernetic systems. Today, Zentaka's subsidiaries span civilian prosthetics, surveillance AI, private security, and consciousness preservation.
